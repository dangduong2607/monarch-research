About Us

Monarch Research is an independent financial research and investment management youth-led interest group founded in 2025 by passionate individuals dedicated to equity and macroeconomic analysis.

Our Mission

We are a collective of young professionals and students committed to conducting in-depth equity research on both technical and macroeconomic levels. Our approach combines rigorous quantitative analysis with insightful qualitative assessment to provide comprehensive market perspectives.
